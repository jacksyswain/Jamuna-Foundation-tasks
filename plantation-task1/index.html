About the Campaign

Jamuna Foundation is leading a tree plantation campaign to fight pollution, improve air quality, and create a healthier tomorrow. Every tree you plant is a step toward saving our planet.

Why Join Us?

🌱 Reduce Carbon

Planting trees helps absorb harmful carbon dioxide and reduces climate change impact.

🌍 Improve Biodiversity

Trees provide shelter and food for birds, insects, and animals, restoring nature’s balance.

💚 Greener Future

Contribute to a sustainable environment for future generations.

Join us for our plantation drive on 15th September 2025 in Hyderabad. Volunteers will receive training and free saplings.
